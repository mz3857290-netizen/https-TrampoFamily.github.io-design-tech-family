The Journey of Design Tech Family

The story of Design Tech begins long before the company itself — it begins with a family rooted in love, trust, and unbreakable bonds.

It all started when Rahimulla Khan married Rasidan Khatoon. A year later, they were blessed with a son, Salamtullah Khan.

One day, while young Salamtullah was working in his father’s fields, a farmer approached Rahimulla Khan. The farmer hesitated at first, nervous to speak, but Rahimulla smiled warmly and said, “Don’t worry, I know everything. You have come to offer your daughter’s hand in marriage to my son. I gladly accept this proposal.” In that moment, Salamtullah felt shy, but his heart was filled with quiet joy.

Soon after, Salamtullah married Nafisa Khatoon. A few years later, the couple was blessed with seven beautiful children: Yahya Khan (1st son), Ayyub Khan (2nd son), Shahnaj Khatoon (1st daughter), Asad Khan (3rd son), Nasreen Khatoon (2nd daughter), Sajid Khan (4th son), and Wajid Khan (5th son).

Today, this legacy continues to blossom with a vibrant third generation — the grandchildren — who carry forward the same values of hard work, unity, faith, and innovation. From rural roots to a modern enterprise, Design Tech stands as a living testament to family, sacrifice, and vision across three generations.

We don’t just build products. We build a legacy.
